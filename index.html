About the Seminar

This special tutorial is held alongside the course Theoretische Physik 4: Quantenmechanik 1 at Goethe University Frankfurt.

The seminar offers a deeper dive into the philosophical and physical interpretations of quantum mechanics. Our main guide will be Travis Norsen's book Foundations of Quantum Mechanics.

Student Projects

Here you will find the list of suggested assignments.

  • Project 1 - Does the wave packet resolve the wave-particle duality?
  • Project 2 - Is the moon there when nobody looks? reality and quantum theory
  • Project 3 - What does decoherence actually do to the measurement problem?
  • Project 4 - How can Born's Rule fail in some worlds of the Many-Worlds interpretation?
  • Project 5 - Does Bell's theorem rely on the experimenter's free will?
  • Project 6 - Electron trajectories in the hydrogen atom according to Pilot-Wave Theory
  • Project 7 - Spin according to Pilot-Wave Theory
  • Project 8 - Is quantum entanglement a wormhole in spacetime? (if you dare!)
  • Project 9 - What are weak measurements?
  • Project 10 - Explore objective collapse theories.
