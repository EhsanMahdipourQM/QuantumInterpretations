About the Seminar

This special tutorial is held alongside the course Theoretische Physik 4: Quantenmechanik 1 at Goethe University Frankfurt.

The seminar offers a deeper dive into the philosophical and physical interpretations of quantum mechanics. Our main guide will be Travis Norsen's book Foundations of Quantum Mechanics.
